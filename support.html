SkyPath is an aviation career planning tool built by a working pilot (ATP, MCFI, Gold Seal CFII/MEI).

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update my hours?

Tap the gear icon on the Roadmap tab to open Settings. Your hours are editable under "Your Hours." Changes automatically update your progress and roadmap estimates.

How do I change my career goal?

Go to Settings and tap "Change Goal." Select your new starting point and goal, then tap "Save & Regenerate" to rebuild your roadmap.

How often is the data updated?

Hiring status, deadlines, and industry news are refreshed every time you open the app and periodically in the background. Scholarship and program data is updated with each app release.

Are the cost estimates accurate?

Cost estimates are based on national averages from flight schools, DPE fees, and testing centers. Actual costs vary by region, school, and individual proficiency. Estimates show a range (low-high) to account for this variance.

I found incorrect information. How do I report it?

Email us at support@grayaerialvisual.com with details about what's incorrect. We take accuracy seriously and will investigate and correct issues promptly.
