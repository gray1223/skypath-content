ClearedDirect Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

Overview

ClearedDirect ("the App") is an aviation career planning tool. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data Collection

Data stored on your device only:

  • Your career profile (starting point, goal, flight hours, certificates held)
  • Your roadmap progress (completed checklist items)
  • Notification preferences

This data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework and optionally synced via iCloud if enabled on your device. We do not have access to this data.

Data We Fetch

The App fetches publicly available content updates (airline hiring status, scholarship deadlines, industry news) from our content server. These requests do not include any personal information, user identifiers, or tracking parameters.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not serve advertisements
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties
  • We do not use cookies or device fingerprinting

Third-Party Services

The App links to external websites (airline career pages, scholarship applications, news sources). These sites have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, the App may send local notifications about upcoming deadlines, hiring status changes, and breaking industry news. You can control these in the App's Settings. No notification data is sent to our servers.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
